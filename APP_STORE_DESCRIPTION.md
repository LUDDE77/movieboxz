## Review Notes for Apple

**About YouTube Integration:**
MovieBoxZ is a discovery and catalog app that complies fully with YouTube's Terms of Service. The app:
- Does NOT embed or play YouTube videos
- Does NOT extract video URLs
- Does NOT host any video content
- Uses deep linking to open videos in the official YouTube app
- Displays clear YouTube branding and attribution
- Shows YouTube requirement disclaimer on first launch

This architecture is required for YouTube TOS compliance, especially on Apple TV where web browsers don't exist.

**Testing Instructions:**
1. Install both MovieBoxZ and the official YouTube app
2. Launch MovieBoxZ - you'll see a welcome screen explaining YouTube requirement
3. Browse movies and tap "Watch on YouTube" button
4. The YouTube app will open and play the video
5. Return to MovieBoxZ to continue browsing

All videos play in the YouTube app. This is intentional and required for compliance.
